Our Horses


The AWE Center has an amazing group of equines that are all talented in teaching and healing humans. They teach us about ourselves and how to become self aware in today's society. Along with the herd's healing abilities, taking riding lessons on any of these horses will teach you sensitivity, balance, and build confidence to a new level.

Eleganta

Eleganta is an 11 year old Lusitano mare. Ellie is kind, compassionate, and good at teaching people how to set boundaries. She likes precision and is great at teaching people all about clarity.


Amiero

Amiero is a 17 year old Swedish Warmblood. He is a 1400 pound love muffin & people pleaser. Amiero is always present with his handler, light, sensitive, and an amazing teacher both on the ground and under saddle.


Salsa

Salsa is a 7 year old pure bred Andalusian mare. She is the newest horse in the herd, Salsa is very kind and smart! She has amazing Liberty training. Salsa is full of energy and just started under saddle training. She is a great addition to our herd.


Romance

Romancé is a 7 year old chestnut Peruvian Paso. He is the kindest, sweetest horses on the farm, but also came with a history of abuse and is highly sensitive. He is one of our best healers because of his life experiences. Romancé likes someone with a gentle soul and a good sense of humor.


Mr.Bigglesworth

Mr. Bigglesworth is a 20 year young Quarter horse (Paint). This horse is a gifted teacher and healer. He is kind, sweet, sensitive, and can teach a person so much about themselves and how to stay present.


Sunny

Sunny is a 30 year old Appendix Quarter Horse with lots of opinions about everyone and everything. We call him "the professor" for his amazing gift of calling things out as he sees them. He's a blessing to have in this herd.
